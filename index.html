    Due to the development of information technology, we need to exploit this development to solve problems and facilitate life. We in our project focus on the graduates sector in terms of training, provide graduates student with a training opportunities in different companies and a way to communicate with these companies. In addition, the system enables the supervisors in the company and the university to track the trainees and monitor their performance during their training in the company. Further, the T P system creates a communication channel between the employers and the student supervisor in order to provide periodic feedback.
